Introduction

The idea is to shoot photographs which are a little out of place or that contain something unique which makes you look a little longer to the photograph. This can be contradictions, an object or subject that doesn't belong in the setting / scene or something that is a little unnatural. Meaning that at first sight (the first second you see the photograph) the picture looks normal, but actually something is very strange or surreal.
